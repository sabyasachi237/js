Welcome to Vital Total Health Medical Group Inc patient Collection Services. Vital Total Health Medical Group Inc is an independent healthcare provider network that provides medical oversight services to you for the laboratory testing that you requested. If your test results are abnormal, Vital Total Health Medical Group Inc Care Coordination Team may attempt to contact you to notify you of your results. You will also have an opportunity to schedule a tele-health consult with an independent licensed physician or other healthcare provider from Vital Total Health Medical Group Inc who can answer any questions you may have about your test results and help determine next steps.
